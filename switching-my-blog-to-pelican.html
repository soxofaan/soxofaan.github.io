Switching my blog to Pelican

By Stefaan Lippens on 2016/08/21
Tagged: pelican, drupal

For several years I had a Drupal powered blog and it served me well. During those days I was an active Drupal developer (working on Drupal modules like CAPTCHA and GeSHi), but that activity and interest has decreased to almost nothing nowadays. My writing activity on the blog decreased also, with only some isolated posts here and there, and an meager revival attempt by re-theming the look.

The whole thing just started to feel like a drag, especially because of the operational and maintenance related burden of keeping it alive. It also felt quite a bit of overkill to use a dynamic CMS system (or whatever it should be called) like Drupal for a basic blog that changes/updates very rarely.

Time to give one of these static site generator tools a try (Pelican of course, being a heavy Python user) and see where it brings us.
