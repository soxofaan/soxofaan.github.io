Hadoop 3 default ports

By Stefaan Lippens on 2018/11/16
Tagged: hadoop

Hadoop comes with quite a bunch of services listening on various ports. For your entertainment I extracted the default port numbers from these Hadoop 3 (default) config files:

  • core-default.xml
  • hdfs-default.xml
  • hdfs-rbf-default.xml
  • yarn-default.xml
  • mapred-default.xml
